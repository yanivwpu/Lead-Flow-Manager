Executive Summary

WATI is a well-established player in the WhatsApp CRM space, but has significant weaknesses that WhachatCRM can exploit. Your customer-owned Twilio model is a major differentiator that addresses many of WATI's biggest complaints.

WATI's Weaknesses (Your Opportunities)

1. Hidden Costs & Markup Fees

WATI charges up to 20% markup on top of Meta's WhatsApp conversation fees. Users frequently complain about:

  • Confusing pricing structure
  • Unexpected overage charges
  • Add-on fees for features, extra users, chatbot sessions
WhachatCRM Advantage: "Bring Your Own Twilio - Zero Markup. You pay Twilio directly at their rates. We never touch your messaging fees."

2. Single WhatsApp Number Limitation

WATI only supports one WhatsApp number per account. Businesses with multiple locations or brands are stuck.

WhachatCRM Advantage: Your Pro plan supports 3 WhatsApp numbers - perfect for multi-location businesses.

3. Difficult Cancellation Process

WATI has no self-service cancellation. Users must email, wait 2+ days, and many report continued billing after cancellation requests. This is a major trust issue.

WhachatCRM Advantage: "Cancel anytime with one click. No emails, no phone calls, no hassle."

4. Limited API Calls

Growth plan limited to 10,000 API calls/month - a bottleneck for growing businesses.

WhachatCRM Advantage: Customer-owned Twilio means unlimited API flexibility based on their Twilio account.

5. Poor Support Response

Users report 1-3 day response times, no phone support, and critical issues unresolved for weeks.
