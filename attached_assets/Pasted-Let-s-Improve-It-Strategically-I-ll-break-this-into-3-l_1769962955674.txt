Let’s Improve It (Strategically)
I’ll break this into 3 levels:
1.	Micro UX polish
2.	KPI improvements
3.	Strategic evolution
________________________________________
1️⃣ MICRO UX IMPROVEMENTS
A. Column Headers Need Revenue Context
Right now they only show count.
Example:
Lead (4)
Better:
Lead
4 contacts
Small copy detail → feels more intentional.
________________________________________
B. Closed Column Is Visually Dead
Closed is empty and pale.
Two improvements:
If empty:
“No closed deals yet.”
Or:
“Drag here when deal is won.”
That subtle instruction helps first-time users.
________________________________________
C. Contacted Column Says “No contacts”
That sounds odd.
Better copy:
“No leads contacted yet.”
Make it stage-specific.
Feels more premium.
________________________________________
D. Follow-Up Badge Needs Stronger Hierarchy
Right now:
•	Date badge
•	Needs Reply badge
•	Status tag
They’re visually similar weight.
Suggestion:
•	Follow-up date = subtle
•	Needs Reply = bold/solid
•	Overdue = strong red emphasis
Because “Needs Reply” is the real action trigger.
________________________________________
