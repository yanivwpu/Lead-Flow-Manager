The Realtor Growth Engine is a high-performance, "done-for-you" automation layer built on top of WhachatCRM. It’s designed to transform a realtor's WhatsApp from a simple chat app into an automated lead-conversion machine.

Here is a breakdown of how it works, how it’s implemented, and how to integrate it:

1. What it Accomplishes for Realtors
Instant Responsiveness: Captures and greets every new lead 24/7 so no inquiry goes cold.
Automated Qualification: Uses AI to score leads based on their budget, timeline, and intent (Buyer vs. Seller), filtering out the "window shoppers."
Consistency: Automatically follows up at 24h, 3d, and 7d intervals if a lead stops responding, ensuring no one falls through the cracks.
CRM Organization: Automatically moves leads through a custom real estate pipeline (New -> Discovery -> Tour -> Offer -> Closed).
2. How it is Implemented (Technical Flow)
The engine is implemented as a workspace provisioning system:

The "Provisioning" Phase: When a user activates the engine, the backend automatically "injects" 8 pre-built workflows, a custom real estate pipeline, and a specialized set of tags and lead fields directly into their private database.
The "Preference" Layer: We created a custom user_template_data system. This allows realtors to change their "Service Territory" or "Follow-up Timing" through the dashboard without ever touching a single line of code or complex workflow logic.
Runtime Execution: Every time a message arrives, the CRM's workflow engine checks if the Realtor Growth Engine is active for that user. If it is, it executes the specialized logic using the realtor's saved preferences.
3. How to Integrate Communication Channels
To get the engine running, the realtor needs to connect their channels in the Settings or Channels tab:

WhatsApp (Primary): The engine is optimized for the WhatsApp Business API via Twilio. Once the realtor connects their Twilio number and Meta Business Manager, the workflows automatically begin intercepting those messages.
SMS: The engine can be toggled to handle standard SMS inquiries using the same lead-scoring and follow-up logic.
Other Channels: Because the engine works at the "CRM Lead" level, once a contact is created from Telegram, Instagram, or Facebook, the follow-up and pipeline workflows will manage them just like a WhatsApp lead.
Activation Steps for Users:
Subscription: Ensure they have an active Pro + AI plan (required for the AI Scoring and multi-step Automations).
Onboarding: The realtor completes the 8-step form to provide their legal business details (required by Meta for WhatsApp approval).
Live Setup: Your team (or the partner) does a quick 1-on-1 to finalize the Meta Business Manager connection.
Go Live: The realtor simply toggles the workflows to "Active" on their Growth Engine dashboard.
This implementation ensures that while the core logic is locked and safe (so users don't accidentally break the automation), they still have the **flexibility to

customize** their territory, keywords, and prices.
